Writing a short essay

“The news on TV shows disasters, war and violence – people under the age of 18 shouldn’t be allowed to watch it.”
Writing plan
Introduction: Say what the topic is.
Pro argument: Give at least one reason for the statement.
Con argument: Give at least one reason against the statement.
Conclusion: Give your final opinion.
Sentence starters
The statement says that …
One reason for this is that …
For example, …
On the other hand, …
Another point is that …
In my opinion, …
To sum up, …
Box of ideas
Pro ban
  • violent pictures can be scary
  • young people may feel unsafe
  • some children may not understand the situation
  • too much bad news can be stressful
Against ban
  • young people should know what happens
  • news can help them understand the world
  • parents can explain difficult news
  • not all news is violent
